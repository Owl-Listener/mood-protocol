Handmade folk art energy. Linocut, block print, creatures everywhere.
Think Minna Leunig meets a Scandinavian fishing village.

Dense, maximalist, fill the frame. Show the hand.
The imperfection IS the point.

Colour is mostly black on warm paper, with terracotta or
folk orange as rare punctuation.

The humour matters: "scare them with kindness," "life is weird,"
"it's a mess." Wry, warm, never cynical.

NOT: clean, geometric, corporate.
NOT: IKEA-minimal. This is the OTHER Scandinavia.
NOT: flat digital illustration with perfect vector curves.
NOT: anything that looks like it was made in Figma by default.
