# Designer Notes

This is where you write your freeform thoughts about the visual direction.
The mood generator reads this file and weights it heavily — your words
here are treated as direct creative direction.

## What to put here

Write like you're briefing another designer. Be specific and evocative:

**Good:** "I want this to feel like a Sunday morning magazine — unhurried,
confident typography, generous white space, warm neutrals. Think Kinfolk
meets Stripe: editorial warmth with technical credibility."

**Less useful:** "Modern and clean."

## Things you might include

- The emotional feeling you're after
- Cultural references (magazines, brands, eras, places)
- Things you explicitly DON'T want
- Relationships between the images you've collected ("the colour palette
  from image 1 but the spacing from image 3")
- Constraints ("this needs to work in dark mode too")
- Audience context ("this is for experienced developers, not consumers")

## Anti-references

Call out what you want to avoid:
- NOT: corporate SaaS dashboards
- NOT: glassmorphic effects
- NOT: dark mode gamer aesthetic
- NOT: overly playful/illustrated

Delete everything above and write your own notes here.
